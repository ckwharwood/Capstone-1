I enrolled in a coding bootcamp to deepen my understanding of web and app development, and I haven't looked back since.

Over the years, I’ve developed a diverse skill set that includes front-end and back-end technologies, as well as experience with database management, cloud services, and version control. Beyond coding, I’m a strong advocate for continuous learning and collaboration. I enjoy contributing to open source projects, participating in coding communities, and working in agile teams where I can share knowledge and grow alongside my peers.
